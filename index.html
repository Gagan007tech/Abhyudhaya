Our Heritage

The Hoysala Spirit

Inspired by the magnificent Hoysala temples — intricate, bold, and timeless — Abhyudhaya celebrates the same spirit of excellence.

What is Abhyudhaya?

Abhyudhaya (Sanskrit: अभ्युदय) means "Rise, Prosperity, and Elevation." It is the annual interhostel sports and cultural competition that brings together students from all hostels to compete, bond, and create memories that last a lifetime.

The theme this year — Hoysala Architecture — draws from the star-shaped stellate temples of Belur, Halebid, and Somnathpur, renowned for their intricate stone carvings and structural brilliance. Like the Hoysala craftsmen, we celebrate precision, dedication, and artistry.
