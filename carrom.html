Singles & Doubles Championship

📅 DateMarch 14, 2026
⏰ Time4:00 PM
Competition Guidelines

Rules & Format

01
The striker used in a game must be brought by the player and certified by the referee.
02
Approval from the referee is required for a change of the striker, only allowed once in a match after a particular board.
03
Powder must be evenly applied before the match begins
04
Applying, wiping, or rubbing powder during the match is not allowed.
05
Players cannot change the position once the board is set.
06
The preliminary games are knockout matches, while from finals, the winners are determined based on the
"Best of 3 Sets".
07
Thumb shots and striking backward are not allowed.
08
Pocketing the striker is a foul. Direct contact with the opponent's pawn is foul.
09
The striker must be in contact with the baselines to avoid a foul.
10
Players are not allowed to hold any solid materials except the striker during the match.
11
The referee's decision is final.
